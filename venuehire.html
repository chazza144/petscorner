Birthday Parties

We offer a range of birthday parties featuring hands on experience with our animals. We can offer exclusive use of our classroom, a chance for the children in the party to meet some of our smaller animals (either 'cute and cuddly', or 'creepy crawlies and snakes') along with a trained member of staff to answer questions. There is also an option of Pets’ Corner goody bags.
Prices:
£7.50 per head - minimum fee of £50 with a £10 non-refundable deposit on booking
£1.50 per goody bag
Payment can be made by cash or cheque.
Pets’ Corner cannot provide food, beverages or decorations for your party, but you are allowed to bring these along, half an hour before the party starts.

Pets' Corner corporate events

Pets' Corner hosts a variety of corporate events, arranging team building days and family petting days.
Please contact us to see how an event can be tailored to your business.

Individual/group corporate sponsorship

Enjoyed your visit, made a new friend or just looking at gifts for friends then why not sponsor one of our animals? Sponsorship is available to anyone who loves animals, from companies to individuals, families or schools. Company sponsorship will promote the company name and logo on the animal’s pen and the company will receive a mention in any publicity regarding the animal.
More information and Sponsorship Application Form (pdf)

Reindeer visits

Why not enjoy a visit from a reindeer to add the wow factor to your Christmas event?
Reindeer make perfect special guests at Christmas bazaars, Christmas lights switch-on, or school/nursery Christmas events.
Prices for one reindeer:
Harlow area £245 Outside Harlow £300 (subject to distance travelling from Harlow)
Corporate events £500+ Due to animal welfare the maximum duration the reindeer can attend an event is two hours and would need to be an outside location preferably.
The reindeer come self-contained with their own pen and handler insurance and risk assessments. Pets' Corner completes the movement licence for the reindeer and provides hand soap.
