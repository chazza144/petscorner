About Us

welcome!

Pets' Corner is a small, friendly farm located in Harlow Town Park. It has a large selection of traditional farm animals, such as rabbits, chickens and ducks, more exotic animals such as Reindeer, Llamas and Alpacas, as well as a number of rare breeds including Red Poll cows, Essex Saddleback and Tamworth pigs.
Admission is free, donations welcome!

School visits

Pets' Corner and the Town Park offer educational packages for a range of ages, linked to the national curriculum. These involve guided tours around the park and Pets' Corner, meeting the animals and learning about the park.
If you're interested in arranging a school visit, all information (and the form to complete) can be found on the Harlow Town Park and Pets' Corner Learning Centre booking form
