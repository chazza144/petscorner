About Us
COME VISIT!

Opened in 1966, Pets' Corner is a small, friendly farm located in Harlow Town Park. It has a large selection of traditional farm animals, such as rabbits, chickens and ducks, more exotic animals such as Reindeer, Llamas and Alpacas, as well as a number of rare breeds including Red Poll cows, Essex Saddleback and Tamworth pigs.
Pets' Corner is free to enter but welcomes donations in the box on site. You can also feed our friends with a bag of pet food from our shop on site and feed vending machines.

THANKING OUR VOLUNTEERS

If it wasn’t for our volunteers we would not be able to do what we do for you... Our wonderful volunteers were honoured this week by our bosses (Harlow Council) who held a special celebration event as part of national volunteers’ week. Our volunteers work alongside our staff to care for our animals. The Friends of Harlow Pets corner volunteers, who run the gift shop and organise fundraising events, were also honoured at the same event.
Thank you to our wonderful volunteers for all the work you do
